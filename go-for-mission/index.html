About Us

Who is Lyson Daud?

Born with a heart for the lost, Lyson Daud started GO FOR MISSION with a clear and bold calling — to step into the remote villages of Mangochi and bring the message of Christ where few others dare to go.

We go beyond preaching. We live among the people — providing hope, practical love, and helping everyone discover the profound purpose of living through faith in Christ.

  • Pure Love and Compassion
  • Unwavering Faith
  • Empowering Communities
Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Guided by faith, driven by love — our purpose is clear.

Our Mission

To reach those who are unreachable. We are relentlessly committed to finding the isolated and overlooked, bringing spiritual breakthrough and practical love to every corner of Mangochi.

Our Vision

Every individual around Mangochi and across Malawi knowing the true purpose of living through Christ — seen, valued, and placed in this world for a divine reason.

Our Values

We operate in love, transparency, and authenticity. We believe every person deserves dignity and a chance to hear the truth that sets them free.

"Finding your purpose through Christ means knowing you are seen, valued, and placed in this world for a divine reason."
