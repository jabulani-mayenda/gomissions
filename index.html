About Us

Who We Are

Go Missions International was established to reach people and places that are dared to be visited. Formed in Mangochi, Malawi — its purpose is to minister unto the peoples of Mangochi and beyond through the love and power of Christ.

Evangelism

Taking the Good News to streets, homes, and the remote villages of Mangochi where others won't go.

Church Planting

Raising up local churches that root themselves in communities and grow disciples for Christ.

Youth Empowerment

Mentoring and equipping the next generation to discover purpose and rise into God-given leadership.

Helping Less Privileged

Standing beside the overlooked — bringing practical love, care, and support to those the world forgets.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Guided by faith, driven by love — rooted in the Word of God.

Our Mission

Go Missions International was established to reach people and places that are dared to be visited. We aim to go out for Christ — ministering to the peoples of Mangochi through evangelism, church planting, and serving the less privileged. Mission stops on the overlooked.

Matthew 28:19–20

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you."

His command is our first concern.

Our Vision

To see people connected back to God. We believe that every soul in Mangochi and across Malawi deserves to know the love and reconciliation that Christ offers — no one is too far gone, no place too forgotten.

2 Corinthians 5:19

"God was in Christ reconciling the world to Himself, not counting their trespasses against them, and He has committed to us the word of reconciliation."

Our Values

We are built on truth. We believe that knowing the truth transforms lives — and that every person, no matter how overlooked, deserves to experience that freedom. We go, we love, we serve, we stand firm in the Word.

John 8:32

"And you will know the truth, and the truth will set you free."

"Aim to go out for Christ — His command is our first concern."

Matthew 28:19–20
